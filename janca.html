About Jana

I am Jana and I would like to confess. At the very beginning I was really struggling with flex-boxes and JavaScript during my Czechitas Academy. It was something completely different from what I had been doing for last few years. To be honest with you, it reminded me of the times I was struggling with Arabic at the uni. But after some weeks it started to be a good fit. The creativity is what appeals to me. And I fell in love with JavaScript…Well, at least, you know…

Speaking about our project, I watched some youtube tutorials about glass morphism style and animation so we could apply them on Hradějov sites. I mean, I really like these styles but of course I do have on my mind its main target group of customers. So we will see.

And we can’t forget the contract owners and their vision, right? They are my former co-workers from the ethnological department from Masaryk University. Roman Doušek wrote those playbooks slash games (they are still at a graphic designer, by the way). And my awesome colleague Marie Novotná who has their business under her thumb. Hopefully it will be a win-win. They gave us text materials and illustrations for the sites and web app and will get amazing websites for free. And we don't have to fabricate original texts and at the same time can use it as an opportunity to deep our knowledge of HTML/CSS and JavaScript.
