Site about our project

You are reading our website about our final project created for Czechitas Digital Academy: Web. Once it is complete it will appear on domain https://hradejov.cz.

It should be a promotional website about playbooks with funny little fairy tale figures called Utrhčíslo, Puzzolente and others.

Being very optimistic it will have a functional e-shop for selling these fabulous playbooks along with a game programmed in JavaScript. To be more specific, it should be a game teaser for start. Fingers crossed. And then, hopefully very soon, the entire version of this game (as a web app) about Utrhčíslo will appear on this site as well.

Our site and it’s design should be appealing especially to children from four to ten years and their parents, grandparents, teachers, and carers. Simply to everyone who wants to explore Brno with little kids.
